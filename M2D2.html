About

American singer and rapper Doja Cat has released three studio albums, one extended play (EP), 32 singles (including fourteen as a featured artist), 25 music videos and three promotional singles. According to Recording Industry Association of America, Doja Cat has sold 12.5 million certified albums & singles in the United States.
